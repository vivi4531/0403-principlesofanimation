Squash and Stretch

Classic example:
The bouncing ball that squashes on impact, stretches when flying.
UI example:
A disappearing dialog box.
Connections:
Often combined with anticipation or exaggeration.
Typical CSS
transform: scale
Pros
Gives a physical feel to elements
Cons
Can look quite cartoonish

...

Example:

Create the bounce and squash classes with keyframes.
Bounce from translateY(0) to translateY(var(--bottom)).
The trick is to only squash a tiny bit, and only during very few keyframes.
